Game tutorial

It is a clicker game. You get pixels when you click!
When you click the 'clicker' (the picture in the middle of Homepage) you will get a point - a pixel.
You can buy improvements listed in store, it will make your clicks more powerful and speed up your pixel collection.
There is a leader board showing you the scores of the best players but you need to sign up to make it to the leader board!
Once you are signed up you can unlock achievements that will show up on your MyAccount page!
Logged in users can also upload their own picture to be used as a clicker! You can do that on your MyAccount page.
Have fun!
